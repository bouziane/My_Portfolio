PADREPIO TV

Padre Pio TV is an Italian television channel that focuses on broadcasting content about the life, teachings, and spirituality of Saint Pio of Pietrelcina, commonly known as Padre Pio. It offers a variety of programs centered around the Catholic faith and the legacy of Padre Pio.

Project Overview

Live Mass Anywhere

Padre Pio TV is a native application for iOS and Android, specially designed to offer more than 93,000 users around the world the possibility of experiencing Mass live. As the creator of this application, I paid special attention to its simple and user-friendly interface, thus meeting the needs of users, especially those over the age of 55. Even for those unfamiliar with technology, the app is easy to use.

In addition, we have integrated a secure payment system directly into the application, using the payment methods available on the iOS and Android stores. This makes it easy for users to subscribe and enjoy all the features offered by Padre Pio TV.
